Etsy

The team in 2014 when we won a National Design Award from the Cooper-Hewitt

Etsy is the global marketplace for unique and creative goods, with more than 50 million items for sale, 2 million sellers, 26 million buyers and $3.25 billion in annual gross merchandise sales. I was a designer at Etsy for almost 5 years, where I have worked on different parts of the product in many capacities. I started at Etsy as a senior designer, worked as a design lead and an interim design manager for a period of time, and finished as a staff designer.
