Etsy

The Etsy design team in 2014 after winning a National Design Award at the Cooper-Hewitt.

Etsy is a global marketplace for unique and creative goods. It is home to more than 50 million items, 2 million sellers, 26 million buyers and $3.25B in annual gross sales. I was a designer at Etsy for almost five years, where I have worked on different parts of the product in many capacities. I started at Etsy as a senior designer, worked as a design lead, and finished as a staff designer.
