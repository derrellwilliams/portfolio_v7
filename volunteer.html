Volunteer work

Beyond my full-time positions, I have enjoyed volunteering my design and front-end development work to local organizations. Two of the non-profits that I've especially enjoyed working with are the Utah Avalanche Center and Hawkwatch International.

Utah Avalanche Center

For around 6 months in 2018 and 2019, I volunteered for the Utah Avalanche Center. As an avid backcountry skier, I use their product daily during the winter months, and having an opportunity to work on a complete redesign for the 2018/19 season has been a wonderful experience for me. You can see the site here or a handful of screenshots below. I worked on all of the design and helped with a large portion of the front-end development for them.

Hawkwatch International

Hawkwatch is a conservation group focused on raptor research around the world as a indicator of ecosystem health. I've helped them with various types marketing collateral including fundraising proposals, calendars and invites.
